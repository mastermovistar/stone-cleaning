About Our Company

Professional stone cleaning london Experts

Stone Repairs and Restoration Ltd stands as Central London's most trusted stone cleaning specialist, delivering premium restoration services from our Covent Garden headquarters. With over 25 years of experience, we've restored countless historic facades and modern stone installations across London's prestigious neighborhoods.

Our comprehensive stone cleaning services include professional York stone cleaning, marble restoration, limestone conservation, and heritage building maintenance. Using state-of-the-art equipment and environmentally-friendly cleaning solutions, we ensure your stone surfaces are restored to their original glory.

Starting from £45/m² for basic stone cleaning to £120/m² for specialist restoration work, we offer competitive rates while maintaining the highest industry standards. Our team of CSCS-certified technicians provides free consultations and detailed quotations for all stone cleaning projects in London.
